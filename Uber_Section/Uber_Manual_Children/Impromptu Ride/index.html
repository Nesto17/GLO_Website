Written and Photo Manuals

Learn by reading through our detail-oriented and carefully curated written and photo manuals.

1. Open the Uber app.

2. Tap the Where To? bar located to the left of the Now button.

3. Type in your desired destination into the Where To? field.

4. Scroll through the vehicle options and select one for the trip.

5. Tap Confirm [VEHICLE TYPE] to indicate your selected vehicle.

6. Make sure your pick up address is correct. If not, tap Search to the right of Your Pickup Location. Search and select the correct pick up location.

7. Tap Confirm Pickup and now you have requested a ride.
