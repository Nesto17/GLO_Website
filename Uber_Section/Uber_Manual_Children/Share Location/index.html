How to Share Live Location with Driver

Video Manual

Learn through our comprehensive and quick video explanation!

Written and Photo Manuals

Learn by reading through our detail-oriented and carefully curated written and photo manuals.

From the Map Screen

1. After being assigned a driver, tap the grey circle icon near the bottom right corner of the map.

2. Tap Confirm.

3.The icon is blue when you are sharing your location with the driver.

4.The icon is grey when you are not sharing your location with the driver.

From the Menu Screen

1. Tap the menu icon in the top left corner of your screen.

2. Select Settings.

3.Swipe down and select Privacy Settings.

4.Click Location.

5.Toggle Share Live Location to the on or off position.
