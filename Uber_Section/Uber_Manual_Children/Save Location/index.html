For commonly used locations such as your home, you may not want to enter the entire address manually every time you use it as your pick-up location or destination. On Uber, you are able to save a location and easily select it for future use.

1. Open the Uber app. Tap on Choose a saved place located at the bottom of the screen.

2. Tap on Add Saved Place.

3. Enter the address or name of the location you wish to save in the Enter an address field and select the location from the drop-down menu.

4. Rename the location to a name that is convenient for you under the Name field. The location will show up as this name under your Saved Places when you select it in the future.

5. Tap Save Place to add the location to your Saved Places. A confirmation message saying Location added to Saved Places will appear.

6. When you want to use a saved location, simply tap the Saved Places button when entering a location. A list of your saved locations will show up. Selecting one of them will automatically fill in the field you were typing in (Pick-up/Where to?)
