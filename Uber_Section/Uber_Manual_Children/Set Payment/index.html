How to Set up Payment

Video Manual

Learn through our comprehensive and quick video explanation!

Written and Photo Manuals

Learn by reading through our detail-oriented and carefully curated written and photo manuals.

1. Open the Uber app.

2. Tap the menu icon on the top left of your screen.

3. In the menu, tap on Payment.

4. Tap the Add Payment Method.

5. You can choose from Cash (Paper Money), Paypal, Credit Card, & Debit Card. Select one of the methods. Then, follow the set of instructions given by the selected payment method.

6. After confirming your pickup location and requesting your ride, a page will appear showing all the available payment methods. Select the method you would like to use.

7. Depending on your location, Uber may be a cashless service. This means that Uber drivers do not accept cash.
Note: Uber Cash is different from Cash (Paper Money)
