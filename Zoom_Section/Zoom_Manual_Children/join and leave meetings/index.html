By entering a meeting ID & password:

1. Open the Zoom application and a window will pop up.

2. Click the Blue Plus Icon above Join.

3. Enter the meeting ID.

4. Below the meeting ID, enter your name for the meeting.

5. Click Join.

6. (Optional) If a password is set for the meeting, enter the password.

7. This will bring you to the meeting.
