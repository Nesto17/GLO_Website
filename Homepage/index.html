The Interact Project!

Interact is a project created and dedicated for the purpose of connecting the modern world of technological advances to the minds of our elders.

Zoom Doordash Uber Venmo

What is Interact Project and its Purpose?

While many are used to the benefits that technology gives us, we should never forget who brought us here and will bring us to the future. Our team shall teach everyone, but especially elders and children, how to use technology, so they could stay home safely and comfortably during COVID.

Through our educational website, we shall break barriers between the youth and elders, and decrease isolation, so that one day everyone in the world, no matter age or any other circumstances, will be equal by enjoying the same benefits of technology.
